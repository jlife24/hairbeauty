our story

Vivian Hair

Hi! my name is vivian. I'm the owner of hair & beauty.

Never in my wildest dreams did I think that I would become a hairdesser. What changed? Kids. I'm sure most parents can relate to the enormous prices you pay just to take your kids to the hair salon. I decided to experiments with my kids & I was amazed by the great feedback I received from strangers, family members & most importantly, the kids. My 3 daughters are 12, 19 & 20 respectively, yet, they still would rather have mommy do their hair.

I started hair & beauty because it was incepted from my passion to help clients who couldn't afford to spend tons of money to go through the salon route. I wanted to help clients craft their ideal beauty standards & showcase their light to the world.
